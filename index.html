More people need help with Health Insurance than you may think..


In 2015, around 80,000 people in Germany had no health insurance cover, which corresponds to 0.1% of the population. This is shown by the results of the supplementary program "Information on health insurance", by the largest annual household survey in Germany.

The experts say that the real number is likely 10 times higher.

Get help with your Health Insurance

We support and advise undocumented migrants, EU citizens, German nationals without health insurance. Our goal is to clarify if access to the regular healthcare system is possible. Our Consultation is confidential and if desired anonymous. Please ask if a consultation in your language is possible.
